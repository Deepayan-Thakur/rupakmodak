Rupak
Modak

Capturing the unseen dimensions of our reality.

Born in Kolkata and based in New Delhi, I am an artist and cinematographer who merges tactile traditions with modern cinematic realms. My foundation in classical architecture deeply informs my visual language; I treat the camera's frame as a spatial construct. Driven by a focus on structure, spatial dynamics, and brutalist forms, I craft moving and still images that explore the stark geometry of light, shadow, and human emotion.

My journey began with paintings and immersive digital installations.
